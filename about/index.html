About Us

We are a family run business bringing homely, wholesome products into the heart of Dublin for over 97 years. We are all hands on with one goal….to deliver good food, freshly made from our bakery everyday.

We are famous for our scones, croissants, muffins, breads, donuts, strudels & much more so you’re welcome to drop by, say hi and take away what you like.

A TRADITION FOR ALMOST 100 YEARS
